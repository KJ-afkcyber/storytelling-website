This is my website for story telling. Once upon a time, a farmer had a goose that laid a golden egg every day. The farmer used to sell that egg and earn enough money to meet their family's day-to-day needs.

One day, the farmer thought that if he could get more such golden eggs and make a lot of money and become a wealthy person.So then he fed that goose a lot of food and did EVERYTHING to make sure it gave him more eggs.The goose did not like this so he decided to run away.

He set off one night without the farmer knowing and never came back.He found a nice place to live and he is now very happy near a pond.THE END
